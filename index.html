Anne Smendinghoff Funeral Procession on April 17th, 2013 at approximately 11:30.

The River Forest Service Club would like to encourage all River Forest residents to show their appreciation for Anne's service to our country and her and her family's ultimate sacrifice for our country by lining the funeral procession route as it leaves the Village. This show of respect has already been approved by her family.

The funeral procession route in River Forest is as follows:


View Larger Map

ETD St Luke Church approximately 11:30 AM, Wednesday, April 17, 2013 proceeding NORTH on ASHLAND AVE to CHICAGO AVE. Then EAST on CHICAGO AVE to LATHROP AVE. NORTH on LATHROP to AUGUSTA ST. EAST on AUGUSTA ST to WILLIAM ST. SOUTH on WILLIAM, passing the family residence to LAKE ST. WEST on LAKE ST to THATCHER AVE. SOUTH on THATCHER AVE. to WASHINGTON BLVD, thence WEST on WASHINGTON to the approaches of QUEEN of HEAVEN CEMETERY. The departure time (ETD) from St. Luke Church is an estimate and the Estimated Times along the procession Route must be estimated accordingly, keeping in mind the lining up of procession cars on ASHLAND AVE as it proceeds NORTH.

We hope that this can be accomplished soon and we have a good turnout from the Village to honor one of our own.

The River Forest Service Club was founded in 1935 with the purposes of promoting the civic welfare of the Village, of advocating honest and efficient government, and of generally promoting the welfare and best interests of the Village of River Forest. Education, fellowship and community service have been the hallmarks of the organization from its inception.
In addition to monthly meetings, the River Forest Service Club sponsors the River Forest Memorial Day Parade, supports community events, activities and philanthropic endeavors, sponsors political candidate forums, and recognizes individuals who have given outstanding service to our neighborhoods, our community, or our country.
