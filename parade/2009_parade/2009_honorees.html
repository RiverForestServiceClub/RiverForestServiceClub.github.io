During the 2009 Memorial Day ceremonies, The River Forest Service Club honored three River Forest residents who have served in the United States armed forces.

Richard M. Cronin, MD

  • Korean War, Chief of Orthopedics at Chanute Air Force Base (1950-1954)
Margaret Martin, Major, USAF (Active Duty)
    Grand Marshall - River Forest Memorial Day Parade (May 25, 2009)
    Experience & Education:
    • Air Mobility Warfare Center, Fort Dix NJ
      • Student, Advanced Study of Air Mobility (2008-present)
    • US Air Force Academy, CO
      • Assistant Professor of History & Director of Human Resources (2007-2008)
      • Instructor of History & Director of Human Resources (2006-2007)
      • Instructor of History (2005-2006)
    • Charleston AFB, SC
      • Ops Gp Executive Ofcr, Evaluator Acft Cmdr, C-17A, 437 OG. (2004-2005)
      • Readiness Fit Cmdr, Instructor Acft Cmdr, C-17A, 16 AS (2003-2004)
      • Instr Acft Cmdr, C-17A, Special Operations Plans, 437 OGS (2002-2003)
      • Chief Sqdn Exec Ofcr, Aircraft Commander, C-17A, 17 AS (2001-2002)
      • Pilot, C-17A, Squadron Executive Officer, 17 AS (2000-2001)
    • Maxwell AFB, AL - Squadron Officer School (2000)
    • Charleston AFB, SC - Strategic Airlift Pilot, C-141B, 16 AS (1998-2000)
    • Del Rio, TX - Student Pilot, Undergraduate Pilot Training (1997-1998)
    • MA, History - College of William & Mary (1997)
    • BA, History - Distinguished Graduate - US Air Force Academy, CO (1996)
    • OPRFHS Graduate
    • River Forest Native
Chester C. McCullough
  • World War II, 26th Infantry Division, Participated in the Ardennes, Central Europe, Northern France, & Rhineland Campaigns
  • Korean War
